Overview

FeatherCat develops small-to-medium ultra-light modular catamarans designed for transport, repair, and configuration across different waters. Early configurations focus on paddling and rowing; optional sailing configurations may follow.

Commerce and memberships will be handled by WaterWolf (private platform). This public site focuses on product information and documentation.
